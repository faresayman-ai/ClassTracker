Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · App: ClassTrack · Contact: faresbarakat289@gmail.com

ClassTrack is a student assignment tracker available at https://faresayman-ai.github.io/ClassTracker/. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and what we never do with it.

1. What data we access

If you choose to connect your Google account, ClassTrack requests access to:

  • Gmail metadata only — email subjects, sender names, and snippets (short previews). We never read the full body of any email.
  • Your Google profile — your name and email address, used only to display your identity inside the app.

2. How we use your data

  • Email subjects and snippets are scanned locally in your browser to detect assignment deadlines.
  • No email content is ever sent to any server or stored outside your device.
  • Your name and email are saved only in your browser's localStorage for session continuity.

3. What we never do

  • We never sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party.
  • We never store your emails or Google account data on any server.
  • We never read full email bodies — only subjects and snippets.
  • We never use your data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data storage

All assignment data you enter is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. If you use the Shared Room feature, assignment data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database only for the duration of the room session.

5. Third-party services

  • Google Identity Services — for optional Gmail sign-in.
  • Firebase (Google) — for real-time shared room sync.
  • Google Fonts — for typography.

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Revoking access

You can revoke ClassTrack's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing ClassTrack.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: faresbarakat289@gmail.com
